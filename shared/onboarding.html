Getting Started

Here's everything you need to know to get the most out of your Tides client portal.

📊 Accessing Your Monthly Reports

Every month, your dedicated bookkeeper posts 4 reports to your portal:

  • Profit & Loss Statement — revenue, expenses, and net income
  • Balance Sheet — what you own and owe as of month-end
  • Cash Flow Statement — how money moved in and out
  • Executive Monthly Summary — plain-English takeaways from our team

Click any month in the Reports section. The files open in a secure Microsoft OneDrive window where you can view them in your browser or download them to your device.

💻 Desktop recommended: Report viewing and downloads work best on a laptop or desktop computer. On mobile, you may need to tap "Download" first.

📤 Uploading Documents to Us

When we request a receipt, invoice, or other document, use the Upload section of your portal. Files go directly to our secure team folder.

Common things to upload:

  • Receipts for cash transactions
  • Vendor invoices not paid through your business account
  • Loan or financing agreements
  • Contractor or W-9 forms
  • Anything we specifically ask for

We're notified instantly when files arrive. Most uploads are reviewed within 1-2 business days.

💬 Getting Help

🔐 Security

Your portal is protected by Cloudflare's bank-grade infrastructure. Each time you sign in, we email you a 6-digit code that expires in 15 minutes. Your data is fully isolated from other clients — no one else can see it.
